About Me

Ruth took a career break and is returning to the workforce. Since Alex has coined the "I'm Boring" title, I have to come up with something else ... How about "Work is fun"?

While I stayed at home to raise my children I kept my sanity by volunteering at their school. Field trips weren't enough so I branched out to applying for grants, handling social media for Council, feeding 730+ kids per pizza lunch, and working with the TDSB to get the Kindergarten yards fixed and playgrounds replaced.

I've worked in or with C, C++, Java, XML, XSLT, Ant, CVS, Eclipse, Unix make files, JUnit, worked with translators for translation testing, managed software development teams, project managed two Eclipse projects (both archived now). There's more but I can't remember off of the top of my head.
