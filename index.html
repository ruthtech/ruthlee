About Me

I am a person who "Gets things done" because I usually find that work is fun. Better busy than bored.

While I stayed at home to raise my children I kept my sanity by volunteering at their school. Field trips weren't enough so I applied for grants, handled social media for Council, fed 730+ kids per pizza lunch, and worked with the TDSB to get the Kindergarten yards fixed and playgrounds replaced.

I've worked in or with C, C++, Java, XML, XSLT, Ant, CVS, Eclipse, Unix make files, JUnit, worked with translators for translation testing, managed software development teams, project managed two Eclipse projects (both archived now). There's more but I can't remember off of the top of my head.
